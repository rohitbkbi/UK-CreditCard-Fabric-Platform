## 3. Business Glossary (selected terms)

- **DPD (Days Past Due)**: number of days a payment is overdue; buckets
  (1-29, 30-59, 60-89, 90-119, 120+) drive collections strategy and IFRS9
  staging.
- **EMI (Equated Monthly Installment)**: a purchase converted to a fixed
  monthly repayment plan, typically at a promotional interest rate.
- **MCC (Merchant Category Code)**: 4-digit code classifying the type of
  business a merchant operates, used for rewards eligibility and risk
  scoring (e.g. gambling MCCs are high-risk).
- **PD (Probability of Default)**: model-estimated likelihood a customer
  defaults within a given horizon; feeds IFRS9 ECL staging (Stage 1/2/3).
- **Chargeback**: a transaction reversal initiated by the cardholder/issuer
  disputing a charge with the merchant's acquirer, per card network rules.
- **Write-off**: an account balance the bank has ceased pursuing for
  recovery internally (may still be sold to a third-party debt buyer).
